Hello! I am Jacob Sykes

I am a Game Developer who enjoys making in depth stories for the games I make.

I believe that some of the best stories are the ones that don’t just outright tell you what is going on, that way you have to put the pieces together yourself as the plot slowly unravels.

I plan on using my technological knowledge to put these beliefs of mine in my own games.
